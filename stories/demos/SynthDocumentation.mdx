# Synths

Synth combinations made with our components

## Single Oscillator Synth

```html
<root-midi></root-midi>
<root-keyboard></root-keyboard>
<root-connect>
  <root-osc id="osc-1" sendTo="filter-1"></root-osc>
  <root-filter
    id="filter-1"
    sendTo="output"
    recieveFrom="osc-1"
  ></root-filter>
</root-connect>
```
